Privacy Policy  
Effective Date: 2025-08-01  
Last Updated: 2025-08-01

Welcome to Reminiscence. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your personal data when you use the Reminiscence mobile application.

1. Overview  
Reminiscence is a mobile app that lets you import and browse your personal Instagram messages using a clean interface and advanced search. All data stays on your device.

2. Data We Collect  
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens on your device.  
We access the ZIP file you export from Instagram only when you choose to load it.  
Your data is stored locally in encrypted form using a password you provide.  
We do not collect usernames, passwords, device IDs, or any identifiers.

3. How Your Data is Used  
Your data is used only to display and search your messages inside the app.  
It is encrypted and saved in a private file format.  
It is never shared or uploaded.  
The original ZIP file is deleted after import.

4. Encryption and Security  
Your password is used to create an encryption key.  
Your messages and media are encrypted and stored locally.  
No data is sent to servers or shared externally.

5. Internet Access  
The app works fully offline.  
Internet is used only to load Instagram reels.  
If offline, that section will not show content.

6. Advertising  
The app currently does not include ads.  
If ads are added in the future, this policy will be updated.  
We will clearly explain any data shared with ad networks if applicable.

7. Children’s Privacy  
Reminiscence is not intended for children under 13.  
We do not knowingly allow children to use the app or provide data.

8. Data Retention  
Your data remains on your device and under your control.  
If you delete the app or remove the .rem file, your data is permanently deleted.  
We do not store data on any remote system.

9. Source Code  
Reminiscence is closed-source.  
The code is not available for use, modification, or redistribution.  
We may allow code viewing in the future for transparency only.

10. Your Rights  
Since we do not store your data, we cannot process requests to access, delete, or correct it.  
All data is fully controlled by you, locally on your device.

11. Changes to This Policy  
We may update this Privacy Policy as needed.  
The most recent version will always appear in the app.  
We recommend reviewing it from time to time.
